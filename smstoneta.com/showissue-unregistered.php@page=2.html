Registered issues with their short codes for sms with their support/oppose details from unregistered mobile

- Below each issue heading is written the SMS-code for supporting the issue (code ending with Y ; Note that there is a space before Y) and the SMS-code for opposing the issue (code ending with N ; Note that there is a space before N) which can be sent to this site (08141277555) after registering with the site.  See More...

Also written are the numerical Support codes (ending with 1) and numerical Oppose codes (ending with 2) for an issue. The numerical codes can easily be sent by less literate persons to this site (08141277555). The numerical codes will convert into their respective alphabetic codes on entering the system. Example - `0011` becomes `TCP Y`,

See how to register at the site and send sms-code to the site here.

- Click on the numerical counts for the Support and Oppose totals for an issue to see all their detailed results with voter id, state and constituency of the registered person giving their opinion for an issue.
